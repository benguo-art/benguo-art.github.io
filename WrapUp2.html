Cube

In cube gamemode, the user appears as their chosen icon and jumps everytime the screen is clicked.

Ship

In ship gamemode, the user appears as their chosen icon and goes up when the user is holding and goes down when released.

Ball

In ball gamemode, the user appears as their chosen icon and reverses gravity everytime the screen is clicked.

UFO

In UFO gamemode, the user appears as their chosen icon and jumps in the air everytime the screen is clicked.

Wave

In wave gamemode, the user appears as their chosen icon and goes up when the user is holding and goes down when released. Unlike the ship, wave swaps direction instantly while ship takes time.

Robot

In robot gamemode, the user appears as their chosen icon and jumps everytime the screen is clicked. The robot can jump higher by holding the screen longer.

Spider

In spider gamemode, the user appears as their chosen icon and reverses gravity everytime the screen is clicked. Unlike the ball, the spider teleports instantly rather than slowly swapping gravity.

Swing Copter

In swing gamemode, the user appears as their chosen icon and reverses gravity everytime the screen is clicked while flying.
