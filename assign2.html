Spider-Man: Homecoming

Spider-Man: Homecoming was released in 2017. It was directed by John Watts and was produced by Columbia Pictures, Marvel Studios, and Pascal Pictures.

After joining the Avengers, Peter Parker returns to high school life while secretly protecting his city as Spider-Man. When a new villain threatens his home, Peter must balance responsibility, identity, and the realities of growing up as a hero. from this website

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Spider-Man: No Way Home was released in 2021. It was directed by John Watts and produced by Columbia Pictures, Marvel Studios, and Pascal Pictures.

Peter Parker's secret identity is revealed to the entire world. Desperate for help, Peter turns to Doctor Strange to make the world forget that he is Spider-Man. The spell goes horribly wrong and shatters the multiverse, bringing in monstrous villains that could destroy the world. from this website
